    My project is on creating a low poly character. My low poly character will be a tall muscular samoan man.

    End User


    My end users are for kids aged from around 8-12 years old.
    Kids around these age are able to do more complicated controls then just basic movements.
    Kids this age would start to enjoy more action in their games and more skill based
