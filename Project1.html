    • Overview

    My project is on creating a low poly character. My low poly character will be a tall muscular samoan man.

    End User


    My end users are for kids aged from around 8-12 years old.
    Kids around these age are able to do more complicated controls then just basic movements.
    Kids this age would start to enjoy more action in their games and more skill based

    Requirements

    Rigging

    This is so the character can have limbs to move

    Movement

    This is so the character can sprint, jump etc..

    Torso

    This is so the character can have a body

    Limbs

    This is so the character can have limbs to move

    • Implication

    Aesthetics

    Aesthetics is all about making something look good. This is really important because
    one of the most important parts of a game is the looks of the character

    I will address this by:

    My character will be a big, buff, muscular dark skinned man who wears
    tight clothes and will also have a nice, fresh buzz cut. He is a
    man who was born and raised in Samoa and his name is Sione.

    Functionality

    Functionality is making a working low-poly character that will do what it is coded to do
    The most important part of the character would be rigging since wihtout that the character
    wouldn't even work/function

    I will address this by:

    This character would be able to do simple tasks such as sprinting, walking, jumping.
    it could possibly do more complicated stuff like vaulting and mantling if possible.

    Usability

    Usability is making a low-poly character easy to use and have possilbe and not too complicated controls

    I will address this by:

    I will make the characters movement smooth and have good, natural running styles other than wonky
    uncoordinated running style/animation style

    Intellectual Property

    Intellectual Property is the rights people have for the thing they created. Which means you cannot use
    it without their permission

    I will address this by:

    My character will not be an ordinary looking person or have a common race for a character.
    My character is a broad, muscular, tall man from Samoa which you don't usually get for video games
